Chapter 1: Breakfast Boosters

1.1. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons granola
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl or glass, layer Greek yogurt and fresh berries.
  2. Drizzle honey over the top.
  3. Sprinkle granola and chia seeds.
  4. Enjoy a protein-packed, low-calorie breakfast!
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 220
Protein: 20g
Carbs: 28g
Fat: 5g

1.2. Spinach and Feta Omelette

Ingredients:
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray
Instructions:
  1. Spray a non-stick skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium heat.
  2. Add spinach and saut√© until wilted.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together egg whites and whole egg.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the spinach in the skillet.
  5. Cook until the eggs start to set, then sprinkle feta cheese on top.
  6. Fold the omelette in half and cook until fully set.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 180
Protein: 24g
Carbs: 3g
Fat: 8g

Chapter 2: Satisfying Snacks

2.1. Protein-Packed Hummus

Ingredients:
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
Instructions:
  1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and cumin.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  3. Season with salt to taste.
  4. Serve with fresh veggies or whole-grain crackers.
Nutritional Information (per 2 tablespoons):

Calories: 60
Protein: 2g
Carbs: 6g
Fat: 3g

2.2. Cottage Cheese and Berry Bowl

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, blackberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon flaxseeds
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, combine cottage cheese and mixed berries.
  2. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle flaxseeds on top.
  3. Mix well and enjoy a refreshing, protein-rich snack.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 180
Protein: 20g
Carbs: 22g
Fat: 2g

Chapter 3: Lunch Delights

3.1. Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette
Instructions:
  1. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken over medium heat until cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per side.
  3. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it thinly.
  4. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  5. Top the salad with sliced chicken and drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
  6. Toss gently to combine and serve.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 350
Protein: 30g
Carbs: 12g
Fat: 20g

3.2. Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 4 slices turkey breast
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tablespoon Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt and Dijon mustard.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and spread the yogurt mixture evenly.
  3. Layer turkey slices, avocado, lettuce, and carrots on top.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roll the tortilla tightly and slice in half.
  6. Serve immediately or wrap in foil for an on-the-go lunch.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 280
Protein: 20g
Carbs: 30g
Fat: 10g

Chapter 4: Dinner Delicacies

4.1. Baked Salmon with Quinoa and Asparagus

Ingredients:
  • 1 salmon fillet (about 6 oz)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Arrange asparagus around the salmon.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon and asparagus, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place lemon slices on top of the salmon.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve the salmon and asparagus over a bed of cooked quinoa.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 450
Protein: 40g
Carbs: 35g
Fat: 15g

4.2. Beef Stir-Fry with Broccoli

Ingredients:
  • 8 oz lean beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
Instructions:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add beef slices and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add broccoli and red bell pepper. Cook for 5-6 minutes, until tender-crisp.
  6. Return the beef to the skillet.
  7. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir to combine and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot, over a bed of steamed brown rice if desired.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 400
Protein: 35g
Carbs: 20g
Fat: 18g

Chapter 5: Delectable Desserts

5.1. Protein-Packed Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
Instructions:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine Greek yogurt, cocoa powder, honey, vanilla extract, and protein powder.
  2. Whisk until smooth and creamy.
  3. Spoon the mixture into serving glasses.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Optional: Top with a few fresh berries or a sprinkle of dark chocolate shavings.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 180
Protein: 20g
Carbs: 20g
Fat: 2g

5.2. Berry Protein Smoothie

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
Instructions:
  1. In a blender, combine almond milk, mixed berries, protein powder, banana, and chia seeds.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
Nutritional Information:

Calories: 220
Protein: 20g
Carbs: 30g
Fat: 5g
